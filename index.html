Quick SGPA

Quick SGPA is a user-friendly, web-based GPA (Grade Point Average) calculator designed to help students effortlessly compute their academic performance. Hosted on Netlify at quicksgpa.netlify.app, this tool simplifies the process of calculating GPA by allowing students to input their grades and credit hours for each course. The application then automatically computes the cumulative GPA, providing instant results.
