My Creative Journey

From NDA Dreams to Architectural Designs

The NDA Inspiration

My artistic journey began with a deep fascination for the National Defence Academy. The discipline, precision, and patriotism I saw in the armed forces inspired me to start drawing military equipment, uniforms, and scenes of valor. This phase marked the beginning of my artistic exploration.

Though my path eventually led me to engineering, the values I discovered during this time - attention to detail, perseverance, and dedication - continue to influence my creative work today.

Architectural Home Designs

After seeing my friend's impressive home designs, I became fascinated with architectural drawing. I began practicing one-point perspective drawings of houses, gradually developing my skills in creating realistic and imaginative home designs.

This new interest combined my technical precision with creative expression, resulting in detailed architectural sketches that reflect both functionality and aesthetic appeal.
